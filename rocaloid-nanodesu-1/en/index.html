Let’s sing with Rocaloid!

Rocaloid provides a new suite of synthesis engine and supporting software for UTAU voice synthesizer; also an integration with Cadencii editor.

After that, Rocaloid will also release utilities for soundbank crafting.

Starting from this version, Rocaloid completed the stage when we develop the technology alone. We will join the UTAU ecosystem and develop the virtual singing stars of our own. Nanodesu!

No matter you are a composer, a producer, an illustrator, a normal audience, or even a researcher or programmer, Rocaloid will continue to power up your wings of imagination!

It will be much practical and interesting. Nanodesu!

Introduction to Rocaloid

Rocaloid is a new suite of synthesis engine and supporting software designed for UTAU voice synthesizer.
